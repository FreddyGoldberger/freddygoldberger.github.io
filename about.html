Welcome to my personal website!

My name is Freddy Goldberger and I am an experienced Electrical Engineer with a background in automation and instrumentation, as well as computer science.

I was born in Bolivia but had the opportunity to study at one of the most prestigious schools of engineering in Chile, the Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María, where I earned my Electrical Engineering degree with a major in Automation and Instrumentation and a minor in Computer Science. For my final project, I designed an IoT system for home automation, which aimed to optimize the use of energy for heating and cooling houses and buildings.

After graduating, I started working for Modular Mining Systems, a Komatsu company, where I joined the team that provides support to the "Autonomous Haulage System". Later on, due to a promotion, I moved to Tucson to join the Global Support team at the headquarters of the company. Recently, I transitioned to the Development Team, taking over the role of manager.

I have a great passion for science and technology, and in particular, everything that has to do with computers, electronics, and control system design. My experience in the field of automation and instrumentation, along with my background in computer science, has given me a unique perspective on the development of IoT systems and other technological projects.

I'm excited to share my journey and knowledge with you on this website, and I hope that you will find it informative and inspiring.
